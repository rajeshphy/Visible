### Logical Reasoning

#### Definition

**It can be defined as the process of using a rational, systematic
series of steps based on sound mathematical procedures and given
statements to arrive at a conclusion.**

-   Logical reasoning is the process of using a rational, systematic
    series of steps based on sound mathematical procedures and given
    statements to arrive at a conclusion.

-   Logical reasoning tests are a broad group of aptitude tests which
    test candidate's problem solving ability.

-   Logical reasoning generally does not require verbal or numerical
    reasoning although variations exist that do.

-   Especially tests that measure sector-specific abilities can have
    verbal and numerical test questions.
