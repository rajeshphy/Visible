    \frametitle{Logical Reasoning}
    \framesubtitle{Definition}
    {\bf It can be defined as the process of using a rational, systematic series of steps based on sound mathematical procedures and given statements to arrive at a conclusion.}
    
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Logical reasoning is the process of using a rational, systematic series of steps based on sound mathematical procedures and given statements to arrive at a conclusion.
        \item Logical reasoning tests are a broad group of aptitude tests which test candidate's problem solving ability.
        \item Logical reasoning generally does not require verbal or numerical reasoning although variations exist that do.
        \item Especially tests that measure sector-specific abilities can have verbal and numerical test questions.
    \end{itemize}
